About us

We help students settle into the school and the city faster than you think

We have created this website to provide new students with helpful tips, useful information, and practical advice to make their university experience stress-free and enjoyable. From finding the best accommodation to discovering the hidden gems of the city, we're here to guide you every step of the way.

Together we can overcome every situation

Our goal is to ensure that you have the best possible start to your university journey and make the most out of this exciting time in your life. So join us at FreshStart and let's make your university experience unforgettable!

We are here to help start this new exciting chapter

At FreshStart, we believe that starting university is an exciting but challenging experience, and we're here to make that transition as smooth as possible for new undergraduates. Our team is made up of current and former university students who have been through it all and know exactly what it's like to navigate the complexities of campus life.

With FreshStart, you'll be able to make the most out of your university experience and create unforgettable memories.
